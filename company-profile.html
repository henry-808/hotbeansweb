About Hot Beans Web

Our Story

Hot Beans Web was founded in 2018 with a simple vision: to build exceptional websites and web applications while nurturing the next generation of development talent. What started as a small team of three developers in a London co-working space has grown into a thriving company with a reputation for quality, innovation, and a genuine commitment to developer growth.

Today, our team of over 25 developers works with clients across the UK, delivering everything from responsive business websites to complex web applications. We remain true to our founding principle — that great developers are made, not born.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver outstanding digital experiences for our clients while providing a supportive, inclusive environment where aspiring web developers can launch their careers. We believe that diverse perspectives build better products.

What We Do

We specialise in building modern, responsive websites and web applications for businesses of all sizes. Our services include front-end development using React and modern CSS frameworks, back-end development with Node.js and Python, database design, e-commerce solutions, and ongoing maintenance and support.

Every project at Hot Beans Web is an opportunity for learning. Our trainee developers work alongside experienced mentors on real client projects from day one, gaining practical experience that no classroom can replicate.
