Eligibility

Find out what we look for in candidates and how the application process works.

What we look for

You don't need to be an expert — but we do need to see the right foundations and the right attitude.

📜 Qualification or Training

A degree, diploma or recognised bootcamp in web development or IT. Relevant self-taught experience is also considered.

💻 Core Web Skills

Solid knowledge of HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Familiarity with a framework like React or Vue is a bonus.

🔀 Version Control

Basic Git skills — committing, branching and pushing to a repository. A GitHub portfolio is a great plus.

🧩 Problem-Solving

You treat bugs as puzzles. You can explain your thinking and work through challenges calmly and methodically.

💬 Communication

You can clearly explain technical concepts and work effectively with both technical and non-technical team members.

How to apply

Our process is straightforward. Here's what to expect step by step.

1

Submit your application

Fill in the application form with your details, chosen role and a short personal statement.

2

Initial review

Our team reviews your application within 5 working days and contacts you by email if you progress.

3

Technical task

A short take-home task (around 2–4 hours) to show off your skills in a relaxed setting.

4

Interview

A friendly 45-minute video call with two team members to discuss your experience and the role.

5

Offer

If it's a match, you'll receive a written offer within 48 hours. Welcome to Hot Beans!
